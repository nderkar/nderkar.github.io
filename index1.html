Environmental Health and Safety

"Companies that aspire to be better environmental stewards invest in strong environmental, health and safety management, otherwise known as EHS. From an environmental standpoint, it involves creating a systematic approach to managing waste, complying with environmental regulations, or reducing the company’s carbon footprint. Successful EHS programs also include measures to address ergonomics, air quality, and other aspects of workplace safety that could affect the health and well-being of employees.”

Our Services

A. Certificate of Registration, Licensing and Consent to Operate Services for Factories, construction premises.

  • Advisory/liaison for Factory license.
  • Advisory/liaison for License from Chief Controller of Explosives (CCE).
  • Advisory/liaison for Labor license by Principle Employer (interstate /State labour).
  • Advisory/liaison for registration and certificate of registration by Employer (interstate /State labour).
  • Advisory/liaison for Certificate of Registration under BOCW act.
  • Advisory/liaison for Registration under EPF and Insurance agencies.
  • Advisory/certificate for OC,NOC of Fire from authority.
  • Advisory/Consent to operate for Air and water from Pollution control Board.
  • Advisory/Authorization for Disposal of Hazardous and Biomedical waste as per statutes.
  • Provide advisory on labor welfare matters.

B. Plans, Procedures and ISO services for Factories, construction premises.

  • Expert in Preparation of Onsite and Offsite Emergency Preparedness plan for factories and construction site.
  • Advising/Drafting OHSE plans, Method statement for critical activities for factories and construction Premises.
  • Expert in developing system for OHSAS 18001, ISO 45001, ISO14001, all training, audits along with certification.
  • Advises on Health and Safety policy with authorized signature from Occupier as per statute with Approved.
  • Expert for Providing safe load calculations for casted slab, scaffold, heavy load erections.
  • Advises on the Occupational accident reporting to concern authority with least litigation.
  • Expert in Preparation of OHSE manuals and Plans in integrated approach.
  • Advisory on Risk assessments/job safety analysis with Projection of training matrix in respect of Hazards.
  • Advisory/Plan for accident free engineering approach for working at height, confine space and Electrical installation with single line load Projection.
  • Advises on accident investigation and root cause analysis by 5 Why method.
  • Advises on single and multidimensional work permit system and procedures.
  • Expert & competent audit team to evaluate fire risks based on Fire Load

C. Tangible Engineering and Training Services for Factories, construction and Domestic premises.

  • Expert in all inspection, examination and testing of all lifting tools and tackles and pressure plants.
  • Provide Live training on Occupational health safety and Environment with national case studies.
  • Providing advisory on mock drill for societies, schools, malls, and hospitals.
  • Organizing Live trainings on fire prevention & fire fighting with actual demonstration at work site.
  • Supplying firefighting equipment’s with annual maintenance facilities.
  • Advising for effective mock drills for fire.
  • Trainings on rescue techniques & basic first aid with the use of AED.
  • Advisory/conduct inspections and examination with fitness certificate of Heavy construction equipment.
  • Facility for Proof load testing of lifting tools & tackles
  • Facility for Nondestructive Testing and NABL Quality lab.
  • Providing qualified & experienced safety professionals as per requirement.
  • Installation of fall protection systems for high-rise building construction projects.
  • Supplying & procurement of safety gadgets.
  • Organizing various motivational programs on various occasions with BBS

D. Specialized authorized training facility offered by PES.

  • Experienced approved trainers who will train the construction team at ground level to sustain the system which will protect the manpower as well as property especially rigging and hoisting, working at height, scaffolding erection & dismantling safety.
  • A team of certified competent person (Approved by DISH in Maharashtra, Gujrat, Goa, Punjab, AP, Telangana, Karnataka, MP, West Bengal, Orissa, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, Delhi, Himachal Pradesh) .
  • Strong back office team to update client concerns about applicable statutory as well as non-statutory requirements which increases/protect brand image of our clients in market.
  • Well-equipped Training center with facility of 5 classrooms with demonstration facilities on Scaffold safety, fire prevention and firefighting, Confined space entry and with many other required equipment which helps participant to understand the concept on respective training topic.
